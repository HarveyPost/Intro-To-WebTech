About Cryptocurrency

Delve into the world of digital currencies and understand its impact on the global financial landscape.

What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security and operates independently of a central bank. It's built on blockchain technology to gain transparency, immutability, and decentralisation.

History of Cryptocurrency

The idea of cryptocurrency began with Bitcoin in 2009, created by an anonymous entity known as Satoshi Nakamoto. Since then, the crypto space has expanded with over thousands of cryptocurrencies in existence today.

How Does It Work?

At its core, cryptocurrencies leverage blockchain technology to maintain a secure and decentralised record of transactions. This technology ensures trust, security, and freedom from central authority.

Types of Cryptocurrency

Beyond Bitcoin, the cryptocurrency world includes significant players like Ethereum, Ripple, Litecoin, and more, each offering different features and technologies.
